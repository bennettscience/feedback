Welcome to OutcomeMaps

This site will help you keep track of your progress developing skills in chemistry. You learn by demonstrating a skill and then taking feedback in order to improve. This account collects all the feedback you've been given so far this year into one place so you can look at your progress.

Grading

Feedback for every assignment this year follows this scale:

N - Not assessable R - Revision needed M - Meets expectations
No submission or so many errors or confusions that the student has not shown the proficiency on the standard being assessed. Your work demonstrates some understanding, but includes errors in processes or concepts which are fundamental to the standard. You have shown a clear understanding of the concept through complete answers with accurate scientific justifications of the standard.

You can click on the box for each standard to see your feedback. Pay attention to how many times you meet expectations compared to how many times you need revision or could not be assessed. You want to meet expectations more frequently than you need revision to show you understand and can do the standard.

Click on your Chemistry class on the left to see your current feedback.
